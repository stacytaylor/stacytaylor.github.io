Nice to meet you.

I’m a product designer specialized in designing developer tools, currently working on identity and access management at Auth0. I also serve on the board of the Computer Human Interaction Forum of Oregon (CHIFOO) helping to organize a monthly speaker series and contributing to the advancement of the local design community in Portland, Oregon.

When it comes to design I love dealing with the messy stuff like overhauling a site’s information architecture, digging deep into a content audit, and improving consistency across a large scale project. I like to take complex problems and make them as simple as possible, but not simpler.

When I'm not geeking out over design I can generally be found reading four books at a time or exploring the Pacific Northwest with my adorably neurotic canine sidekick.
